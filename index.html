Core Feed Engine

These are the features users should trust first. They are the foundation of FB-Refiner and work immediately on Facebook feed pages.

🚫 Block Ads & Suggestions

Removes sponsored posts, feed recommendations, and promotional clutter so your feed feels cleaner and more relevant.

  • Removes ads and sponsored content
  • Hides suggested posts and pages
  • Makes feed browsing smoother

🎬 Hide Reels Section

Turn off the Reels experience completely and keep your browsing focused on actual posts.

  • Removes Reels panel from Facebook
  • One-click toggle in the floating menu
  • Stops the endless scroll trap

📝 Keyword Feed Filter

Type your own keywords and hide feed posts that contain the topics you want to skip.

  • Custom keyword filtering
  • Live filtering in the floating UI
  • Works for feed posts, not comments

Comment Safety (Experimental)

These tools are experimental and improve with high-quality community dictionaries. They assist you while you interact, but are not a full moderation solution.

☝️ Hold Your Finger

Review your comment before posting. The extension checks drafts against the lists you upload.

  • Detects words in your dictionary
  • Shows the matched word
  • Offers a chance to edit before sending

☣️ Filter Toxic Comments

Automatically hides visible comments that match your custom lists while you browse.

  • Hides comments in real time
  • Depends on your uploaded words
  • Works best with strong dictionaries

🔍 Comment Search

Search comments instantly and highlight matches without scrolling through long threads.

  • Search within visible comments
  • Real-time highlighting
  • Fast and responsive
Transparent warning: These protection features are not a replacement for moderation. They only catch terms that appear in your custom dictionaries, so use them as a helpful assist, not a guarantee.
  • Quick tip: You can add words directly from Facebook using the extension's right-click context menu. Select text → Right click → "Add to FB-Refiner (Hard/Soft)" to save words instantly.
  • Where it appears: Words added this way show under Extension Settings → Context Menu lists.

Quick Start

Get FB-Refiner working in seconds with the recommended setup path.

1. Open the floating menu

Click the small FB icon on Facebook to open the extension panel.

2. Enable core filters

Turn on Block Ads, Block Reels, and Keyword Filter for the best feed experience.

3. Optional upload lists

If you want comment protection, import your own dictionary files from the extension settings.

Best practice: Use the extension first for feed cleanup, then only add list-based protection if you want extra filtering. The main feed tools are the priority.
